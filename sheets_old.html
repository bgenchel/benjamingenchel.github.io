Volatile (2018)

I frequently work jazz lead sheets in my research, leading me to write this short piece in traditional AABA structure. It is called, "Volatile", because I feel that in both sections it shifts from pleasant and consonant sound to dark and dramatic.

Transcriptions

You're Cute (Solo) - Tennyson
(2018)

Tennyson is a Canadian electronic music duo, and one of my favorite artists. Their music is unbelievably detailed, making clever use of processed environment sounds and stereo field to create lush soundscapes which frame beautiful harmonies and melodies.

"You're Cute", one of their earlier works, was the first Tennyson song I heard; the solo has always stood out to me.

Solo starts ~2:38
