About Me

Hi! My name is Benjamin "Benjie" Genchel. I am a 24 year old computer scientist, musician and general enthusiast of math, science, art, and people from Southern California. I am always interested in learning something new, and using my knowledge to create.

I became interested in math and engineering in high school thanks to some fantastic mentors and teachers, which led me to pursue my undergraduate degree in those subjects while at UC San Diego. I entered as a mechanical engineer, quickly switched to electrical engineering then later began focusing most of my energy into programming and computer science. I am especially interested in applications to machine learning/deep learning, signal processing and neuroscience.

I began playing drums when I was 13, sparking a love for music that has persisted and grown since. I played jazz drums throughout high school and my undergraduate degree. I am also a self-taught guitarist, partially self-taught pianist (I began taking jazz lessons in 2015), and professional car singer. I have produced several electronic pieces in Ableton Live and FL Studio since my freshman year of undergraduate and began taking production lessons in 2016. Music to me is an endlessly fascinating medium for novel expression, as well as a powerful force for promoting collaboration and understanding.

My other interests include animation, drawing, fashion and reading. I read extensively about favorite cartoons and comics, doodle constantly, imitate several voices, scan through fashion catalogs and always have a book or two in my Amazon Audible queue and next to my bed.

I am currently working as a software engineering intern while applying to graduate programs in music technology, an attempt to combine and explore the intersection of my favorite things. If you'd like to learn more about what I do and what I've done, check out my portfolio page and resume! I am always open to opportunities, meeting people and sharing interests so please feel free to reach out to me through my Contact Me page, or any of the social networks shown above :)
